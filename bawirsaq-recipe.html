Bawirsaq

Boortsog or bawïrsaq is a type of fried dough food found in the cuisines of Central Asia, Idel-Ural, Mongolia and the Middle East. It is shaped into either triangles or sometimes spheres. The dough consists of flour, yeast, milk, eggs, butter, salt, sugar, and fat.

prep time

5 min.

cook time

35 min.

serving

6 servings

Tags : balic bawirsaq becbarmaq nawriz koje

instructions

step 1

Heat 1/4 cup of milk and 1/4 cup of water(just make them warm). Pour warm milk and water in a bowl. Add 1 tablespoon of yeast to them and let it rest for 15 minutes. Add 1 tablespoon sugar, 1 tablespoon butter or vegetable oil, and ¼ teaspoon salt to the bowl and stir them.

step 2

Add 1 egg and stir, pour 2 cups of flour step by step (do not pour whole flour) and combine them to make a dough. Continue pouring flour till making the dough. Grease a bowl and put the kneaded dough in it. Cover the bowl with plastic wrap and put a cloth on it. Let it rest in a warm place for 45 minutes.

step 3

After 45 minutes, transfer the dough on the floured surface. Then sprinkle a little flour on dough and make it thinner into 0.5 cm. Cut the dough into circular pieces or any shapes you prefer.

step 4

Heat 2 cups of vegetable oil in a pan, then drop dough pieces into the oil. The heat under the pan should be medium. When the color of the Baursak changed to a golden brown, turn it to another side and fry it too.

step 5

Place fried Baursak on the paper towel.

ingredients

Milk(warm): 250ml

Sugar: 4 teaspoon

Yeast: 1-2 teaspoon

Salt: 1-2 teaspoon

Cooking oil: 20ml

Sour cream(Yogurt): 2 teaspoon

Flour: 300-500gr
